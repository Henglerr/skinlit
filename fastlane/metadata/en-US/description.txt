SkinLit gives you cosmetic skin-scoring feedback from a selfie and helps you track progress over time.

What you can do in SkinLit:
- Capture or choose a clear selfie for cosmetic analysis
- See your overall score and criterion breakdowns
- Compare scans over time and follow progress
- Unlock deeper analysis, routine ideas, and history with SkinLit Pro

SkinLit is designed for cosmetic wellness insights only. It is not a medical diagnosis tool and does not provide treatment advice.

You can start locally without creating an account. Sign in with Apple or Google is optional and is used for Cloud Save Beta and referral features.

SkinLit Pro is an auto-renewing subscription managed by Apple. Purchases can be restored inside the app, and subscriptions can be managed or canceled from your App Store account settings.
