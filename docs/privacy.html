Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-27

1. Who we are

SkinScore is a cosmetic skin analysis and progress tracking app.

2. Data we process

  • Account data: sign-in provider, user identifier, email/name when provided by Apple or Google sign-in.
  • Onboarding data: skin profile preferences selected in the app.
  • Analysis data: score, criteria, and summary generated from your selected/captured photo.
  • Subscription data: purchase and entitlement status provided by Apple StoreKit.

3. Photos and analysis

Photos are used only to generate cosmetic analysis results. By default, analysis can run on-device. If a cloud analysis endpoint is configured by the app operator, the selected image may be sent securely to that endpoint for processing.

4. Storage

App profile and analysis history are stored locally on your device. Session and authentication state are stored securely using iOS Keychain.

5. Notifications

SkinScore may send local reminder notifications on your device to help you finish onboarding and return for follow-up scans. Notification preferences are controlled through iOS Settings.

6. Tracking and advertising

SkinScore does not use third-party ad tracking or cross-app tracking.

7. Your choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can delete your account from within the app.
  • You can manage camera and photo permissions in iOS Settings.
  • You can manage notification permissions in iOS Settings.

8. Contact

For privacy questions, use the support page: Support.
