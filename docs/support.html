Support

Response target: 2-5 business days

Need help?

For bugs, billing help, or account requests, use one of the channels below:

  • Email: support@skinscore.app
  • Privacy requests: include Privacy Request in the subject line

Include in your request

  • Device model and iOS version
  • App version
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible

Account and data

For account deletion or privacy-related requests, email support@skinscore.app and include "Privacy Request" in the subject line.
